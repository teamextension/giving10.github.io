Today, as many as 7,000 people, mostly children, die every day from malaria. According to Project Concern International, Malaria is the number one killer of children under five in Ghana, and the highest cause of morbidity among pregnant women. In children under five years, 44.3% of all diseases are due to malaria while it contributes to 22% of under-five mortality.
