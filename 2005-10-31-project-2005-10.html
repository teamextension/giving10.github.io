These children will have come from other orphanages or directly from the streets. Olena Ivanova Baran, for instance, is finishing the 11th grade there. Ukraine is one of 18 countries that experienced a decline in its Human Development Index between 1990 and 2003, in large part as a result of the economic changes that resulted after the break-up of the former Soviet Union. According to UNICEF, children often bear the brunt of the poverty: "some mothers cannot cope and either abandon their children or give them up to orphanages.
